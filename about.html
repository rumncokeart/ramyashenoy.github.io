About the artist

Ramya Shenoy is a contemporary figurative painter whose work explores feminine defiance, cultural memory, and quiet transformation. Drawing on both traditional South Asian visual language and modern sensibilities, her portraits seek to capture the subtle power and layered emotional landscape of her subjects. With a background in both technology and the arts, Ramya fuses structure with symbolism, boldness with tenderness, and narrative with nuance.
